How it works

Pomodoro Quest is an offline-first Pomodoro timer paired with task tracking and simple points-based motivation.

Tasks

Create tasks with planned pomodoros. Select a task before starting a work session so your completed pomodoros count toward that task.

Timer

The timer cycles through Work → Short break, and schedules a Long break every N pomodoros (configurable in Settings).

Points & analytics

Completing a work session awards points and records a daily total so you can see your trend over time.

Accounts (optional)

You can use the app without signing in. If you create an account and sign in, your tasks, settings, and analytics are stored under your profile on this device. This build does not sync to a server.
